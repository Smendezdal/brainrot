
"Welcome to Brainrot Web, an innovative application designed to enhance your reading experience. With Brainrot Web, you can easily upload text files and have them read out loud directly on the webpage. Our application leverages the power of React for a seamless user interface and Flask for efficient backend processing. Simply upload your text file, and our system will convert the text to speech, providing you with an audio playback that you can control. Brainrot Web is perfect for those who prefer listening to reading or need assistance with text-to-speech conversion. Try it out today and transform the way you consume written content!"
